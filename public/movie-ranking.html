The Best Movies According to Shivam

My top 3 movies of all time.

Forrest Gump

Teaches you what life is all about, and anyone can live a wonderful life.

Shawshank Redemption

Another masterpiece on life and how life revolves around those who you spend your time with. You can survive and overcome the toughest of times with skills you've aciqured in your lifetime.

The Green Mile

Shows how a gift from God can be a boon to others but curse for the person having it, really makes you cry towards the end.
